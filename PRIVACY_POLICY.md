# Privacy Policy for NutriLens

**Effective Date:** [Add Date]

## Introduction

NutriLens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name
- **Authentication Data:** Encrypted passwords (via Firebase Authentication)

### 2. Nutrition Data
- **Food Entries:** Photos of meals, nutrition information, meal times
- **User Goals:** Calorie targets, macro goals, personal health information
- **Progress Data:** Daily logs, streak information

### 3. Camera and Photos
- **Camera Access:** Used solely to scan food for nutrition analysis
- **Photo Library Access:** Used to select existing photos for analysis
- **Image Data:** Food images are sent to Anthropic Claude AI for analysis, then stored in Firebase

### 4. Technical Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features used, crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

- **Nutrition Tracking:** To analyze food images and log nutrition data
- **AI Analysis:** Food images are processed by Anthropic Claude AI to identify food and estimate nutrition
- **Account Management:** To authenticate users and sync data across devices
- **Service Improvement:** To improve app features and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Firebase:** User data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication)
- **Anthropic:** Food images are sent to Anthropic API for AI analysis
- **Encryption:** All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
- **No Selling:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties
